Adequate Sleep:

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night to support recovery, mental health, and overall well-being.

Stay Hydrated:

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to keep your body hydrated and support overall function.

Balanced Diet:

Eat a variety of nutrient-dense foods, including fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, whole grains, and healthy fats.

Regular Exercise:

Engage in at least 150 minutes of moderate exercise or 75 minutes of vigorous activity per week, such as walking, cycling, or strength training.
